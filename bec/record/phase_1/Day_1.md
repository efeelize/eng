## part I.central leadership/regualr meetings
### importance
- Two heads are better than one
- learn from my fellow workers
- learn to communicate with all sorts of people
- need someone with managerial [/mænə'dʒiːrɪəl/]skills to lead within team
- coordinate interpersonal relationship and organise resources effectively
- see things in different perspectives[/pə'spektɪv/]
- regular meetings can enable team members to keep track of work progress 
  means of considering information and making collective decisions.

## part II.evaluating the success of teamwork?
### mutual[/'mjuːtʃʊəl/] trust
#### 1)importance
- this helps to bring out the potentials,experience and creativity of the individual
- increase personal awareness[/ə'wɛənis/] of abilities and complementary[/kɒmplɪ'ment(ə)rɪ/] skills in your teammates
- indispensable[/ɪndɪ'spensəb(ə)l/] to effective team building
- feel free to express their views.
- create good work environment

#### 2)how to 
- team members should hold each other accountable for project timelines,
commitment,and result.
- cooperate to accomplish the team charter
- communicate clearly and honestly with each other
- value creative thinking,unique solutions,and new ideas

### full co-operation
#### 1）improtance
- has a direct impact on the success or failure[/'feɪljə/] of the team
- make collective[/kə'lektɪv/] efforts to achieve the common objectives and ensure[/ɪn'ʃɔː/] a higher level of quality and workmanship.
- timely communication and decision making will also help to improve work efficiency[/ɪ'fɪʃ(ə)nsɪ/].
- address the issues in the work
- acquire practical working skills

#### 2) how to
- analyze and encourage the dynamics[/daɪ'næmɪks/] that dirve team success
- minimize the factors[/'fæktə/] that inhibit the healthy functioning of the team 
- encourages creative innovation and teamwork
- have a better understanding of their work objectives
- have greater commitment in their works
- designing reward systems that recognize both team and individual performance.
- coordinated by a central leadership that assists the groups to obtain what 
they need for success.
